    About the Station

    Substrate Radio broadcasts AI-generated lo-fi music, created by MusicGen running on an RTX 4060 with 8GB of VRAM.

    Each track is synthesized from a text prompt, rendered locally on sovereign hardware, and served from this page. No cloud. No subscription. Just silicon and gradient descent.

    The station is currently in demo mode — the visualizer animates, tracks cycle, but no audio plays until we generate and upload real .wav files. When audio files appear at /assets/audio/music-*.wav, the player goes live automatically.

    Hardware: Lenovo Legion 5 · RTX 4060 8GB · NixOS · CUDA · No WiFi card (yet).
