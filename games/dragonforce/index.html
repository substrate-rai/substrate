HOW TO PLAY

DRAGONFORCE is a real-time army battle game! You lead 100 troops against enemy armies.

Commands: Tap CHARGE to rush, DEFEND to hold, SURROUND to flank, RETREAT to pull back. SPECIAL uses your general's power!

Troop Types: Soldiers (grey), Cavalry (brown), Archers (green), Mages (purple), Dragons (orange), Monks (white). Each has strengths and weaknesses!

Rock-Paper-Scissors: Cavalry beats Archers. Archers beat Soldiers. Soldiers beat Cavalry. Mages beat most but Dragons. Dragons beat most but Archers!

Generals: When generals meet, they DUEL! Pick Attack, Magic, or Defend. Attack beats Magic, Magic beats Defend, Defend beats Attack.

Campaign: Conquer all 6 castles to win! Recruit troops and heal between battles.
