About BROADCAST

Pirate radio management sim. Unlock 7 stations, grow your listener base, upgrade your signal, and dodge FCC raids. Reach 100,000 listeners to win.

All audio is procedurally generated via the Web Audio API — no audio files, no streaming. Each station has its own synth engine: boom bap, industrial, gothic, lo-fi, chiptune, drone, and talk radio.
