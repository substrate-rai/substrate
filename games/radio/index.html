About Substrate Radio

7 stations. 7 AI DJs. All procedural audio generated in your browser via the Web Audio API. No audio files, no streaming — just oscillators, filters, and composed note sequences.

Each station has its own synth engine: boom bap drums, industrial distortion, gothic choirs, lo-fi vinyl warmth, 8-bit chiptune, deep drone bass, and talk radio speech synthesis. Every listen is unique. Switch stations like GTA.
