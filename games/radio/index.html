    About the Station

    Substrate Radio generates procedural lo-fi music entirely in your browser using the Web Audio API. No audio files, no streaming, no cloud — just oscillators, filters, and math.

    Each station uses layered sine, triangle, and sawtooth oscillators with low-pass filters, LFO modulation, feedback delay, and vinyl crackle. Notes are drawn from pentatonic and minor scales, randomized per session. Every listen is unique.

    Five stations, five moods: chill, dark ambient, glitch, warm, and cosmic. Hit play.

    Hardware: Lenovo Legion 5 · RTX 4060 8GB · NixOS · CUDA · No WiFi card (yet).
