myco world

A structured path to Claude fluency for people without pedigree. Not tutorials — opinionated guidance on what to learn, in what order, and why it matters for your goals.

the gap

Anthropic has a free academy that teaches Claude products — API, Claude Code, MCP, prompt engineering. It's organized by product, not by outcome.

A layperson staring at 13 courses has no idea which ones matter for what they're trying to do, no sense of sequence, and no community to learn with.

Myco World fills the gap between "I've heard of Claude" and "I can use Claude to meaningfully change my work or career."
